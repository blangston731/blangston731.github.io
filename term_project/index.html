Welcome Adventurers!

We are Salmon River Adventures! We specialize in fun, excting, and sometimes thrilling river adventures. We welcome all skill levels to try rafting and kyaking with us. We have certified river guides to show you the ropes and help create a wonderful memory. We offer different lengths of trips as well. You could go for a day or make a week trip out of it. Feel free to look at the trip options below and reserve your spot now! We look forward to our next adventure with you!

Single Day Trip

Skill Level: Beginner to Intermediate

This is a great trip for a family. You can have a day of fun on the river. There are two different route options, determined by skill level. We provide life vests, helmets, the raft, oars, and lunch! There are quiet parts of the river where you can jump out and swim for a little, but also some fun rapids to takle as a group. We ask that childeren are over the age of 10.

Weekend Trip
(3 days, 2 nights)

Skill Level: Intermediate to Advanced

For this trip, we recommend having been on a few whitewater trips in the past. There are rapids on this trip and some area that can be a little rough. This makes for an exciting trip! Our guides will be there with you every step of the way. We provide life vests, helmets, the raft, oars, lunches, dinners, and breakfast(for two days).

Book Now

Week Long Trip
(6 days, 5 nights)

Skill Level: Advanced to Expert

This trip is for the thrill seekers with some experience. The whitewater is rougher than our other trips we offer. The experience is amazing! Camping under the stars at night and exhilarating rapids during the day. We usually have two guides go on these trips with two rafts. This helps with the gear needed. It is a wonderful trip that you will not forget.
