Geyser Day!

Geyser it is a day for locals to come together and see the beautiful Soda Spring! This event was established about 10 years ago. Over the years it has drawn tourist to come and participate as well. Early in the morning, around 8:00am, people begin to gather together to see the geyser. The mornings are still a little chilly, so jackets and blankets were fairly common. The mayor was present as well and began doing a count down as the time for the eruption drew closer. The atmosphere became charged as others joined in on the countdown.
